Unit Paintings, one and twelve is grounded in the creation of a vernacular of the visual. It is composed of units or marks that when juxtaposed and reconstructed at once create and negate visual communication. The work consists of twelve paintings on panels that are intended to be actively reconfigured in a constant curation of new arrangements and compositions. The work can be seen as a series of twelve paintings, a single painting, or any combination of panels.
